Fragments of Me is a digital photobook that captures my journey from birth to age 20, presented as an interactive timeline of memories.

On the right side of the site, a vertical timeline allows you to navigate through the years, bringing each era to life as you click on a specific date. With each selection, the corresponding photo and its story appear, revealing reflections unique to that time in my life. As you explore, music popular during that period begins to play, immersing you in the sounds of the era and adding a sensory layer to the experience.

The site blends scrapbook-inspired visuals, with torn edges and aged textures, with interactive design to make each memory feel tangible. This collection invites you to reflect on how moments shape who we are, offering a glimpse into the passage of time while inspiring you to consider your own fragments of memory and growth.
