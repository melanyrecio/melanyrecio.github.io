Ezo Kitchen & Sushi Bar

Our Story

Ezo Kitchen & Sushi Bar was born out of chef Michael’s deep respect and admiration for Asian cuisine. Having traveled extensively through Japan, Vietnam, and Thailand, Michael returned to Long Island with a mission: to create a dining experience that celebrates Asian food culture while engaging and educating the local community.

We collaborate closely with chefs and culinary experts from each of these countries to ensure every dish remains true to its origins. At Ezo, we believe in cultural respect and delicious food, served with heart.

Meet Our Chefs

Head Chef/Founder

Hi! My name is Michael. My passion for Asian cuisine began with my travels across Japan, Vietnam, and Thailand. After years of studying authentic techniques and recipes, I opened Ezo Kitchen & Sushi Bar to bring my culinary experiences to Long Island. With a focus on sushi and pan-Asian flavors, I am committed to delivering a dining experience that respects the cultures behind the dishes.

Sous Chef

Hi! My name is Sarah. I honed my sushi-making skills under the guidance of traditional Japanese sushi chefs. My precision and attention to detail are what make my sushi creations not only beautiful but incredibly delicious. I believe that every roll should tell a story, combining artistry with fresh, seasonal ingredients.

Chef de Partie

Hi! My name is David. I bring a creative edge to the kitchen, blending Thai, Vietnamese, and Japanese flavors in innovative ways. My playful approach to fusion cuisine, combined with my deep respect for tradition, ensures that every dish strikes the perfect balance between bold flavors and authenticity.
